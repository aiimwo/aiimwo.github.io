About

Aiimwo's practice begins without a predetermined concept or message.

Each work starts as an open process, guided by intuition rather than a fixed planned outcome. As it develops, a direction gradually reveals itself. From that point, the work is refined until it reaches its natural resolution.

The resulting images are not intended to dictate interpretation. Instead, they preserve the trace of a singular creative moment that cannot be repeated in exactly the same way.

Only when a work is complete does its title become clear—not as an explanation, but as the final recognition of what it has become.
